ABOUT ME

As a passionate model, I have been fortunate to participate in several competitions and showcase my talents to the world. I am proud to say that I have been successful in these competitions, winning top honours and earning recognition from industry experts. In addition to my competition successes, I have also had the opportunity to work as a brand ambassador for Jeep, representing the brand at an auto show. This experience allowed me to showcase not only my modelling skills but also my ability to connect with audiences and effectively promote a brand. My skills and talents as a model are rooted in my dedication to hard work, attention to detail, and ability to take direction. I have honed my skills through years of training and practice, constantly pushing myself to improve and refine my craft.

But what truly sets me apart as a model is my ability to bring a unique perspective and energy to every project I take on. Whether I am walking the runway, posing for a photo shoot, or representing a brand as a spokesperson, I always strive to bring my own personal touch to every job. I believe that my skills and experiences as a model make me an ideal candidate for any position in the industry. I am confident in my ability to excel in any challenge and look forward to the opportunity to bring my talents to a new role.

I enjoy activities such as acting, modelling, and walking on runways. I love the thrill of performing and the excitement of being in front of an audience. I always relish the opportunity to express myself through these creative outlets.

Another thing about me is that I love animals, dogs. They're so loving, and always manage to bring a smile to my face. Whether it's playing in the park or cuddling up on the couch, spending time with them is one of my favourite things to do.

Finally, I enjoy doing dancing, singing, playing racquet sports, specifically badminton; Also, I enjoy thrill-seeking activities like riding bicycles, hiking, riding roller coasters and taking adventurous trips.
